The Story Behind Every Stitch

My love for sewing began in my hometown of Takeo, Japan, where I spent countless hours learning from my grandmother. Watching her transform simple pieces of fabric into something beautiful fascinated me. She taught me that sewing wasn't just about making or repairing clothes — it was a way to express creativity, care, and love.

As I grew older, that curiosity became a lifelong passion. Every stitch reminded me of the time we spent together, and those memories continue to inspire everything I create today. The excitement I felt as a child is still with me, and I hope each handmade piece brings that same sense of happiness to others.

The Birth of Akabi Select Boutique

To pursue my dream, I moved to Fukuoka to study fashion and design, where I learned new techniques and developed my own creative style.

In 2012, I founded Akabi Select Boutique with one simple goal: to create handmade accessories that make people smile. Every item is thoughtfully designed and carefully sewn by hand, combining the craftsmanship I learned in Japan with the joy of creating something unique.

Thank you for supporting my small business and allowing me to share my passion for sewing. I hope every piece you receive is filled with the same love, care, and excitement that inspired me to create it.
